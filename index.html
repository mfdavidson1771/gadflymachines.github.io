Dialectic as a Way of Building

What is it?

Gadfly Machines is a workshop where dialectic becomes design — a space where humans and machines reason together to build tools, systems, and services for human flourishing.

We’re currently in alpha.

Focus Areas

  • Traditions of Thought
    We use the Socratic method and its derivative ways of pursuing knowledge, both to understand and to build.
  • Tools That Question Back
    We explore how machines, using artificial intelligence and machine learning, can employ dialectical methods to produce products and services for human flourishing.
  • Learning Systems, Rethought
    Beyond platforms or classrooms, we ask what new shapes learning might take when rooted in wonder, critique, and clarity.
  • From Provocation to Prototype
    We follow ideas from first questions to first tools — where dialectic becomes design.

Current Status

Alive. Early-stage. Privately building.
